Privacy Policy

No personal information is collected by me without your explicit consent. This can include your email address and name, and any details you choose to share via the contact form on the website, in-app contact form, or support email addresses. We will never use this information for marketing purposes, or share it with third parties, and will destroy any information we hold on you at your request via support@yoctoapps.com.

I do use a number of trusted third party services to give you the best experience, these include:

Apptentive, which provides a service to manage customer feedback and ratings, and in doing so collects some anonymized app usage information.

http://www.apptentive.com/privacy/

Crashlytics, which provides information on application crashes, as well as anonymized usage information, and conversion tracking.

https://get.fabric.io/crashlytics#Privacy
